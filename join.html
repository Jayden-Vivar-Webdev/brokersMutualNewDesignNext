Requirements

Membership Requirements

To ensure we can provide the highest level of service, we have minimum requirements for new members.

Minimum Investment

We require a minimum initial investment of $25,000 to open an account. This ensures we can implement an effective diversified strategy for your portfolio.

Identification

You'll need to provide government-issued photo ID (driver's license, passport) and proof of address (utility bill, bank statement) to verify your identity.

Advisor Meeting

All new members complete an initial consultation with a financial advisor to discuss goals, risk tolerance, and investment strategy.

Agreements

You'll need to sign our client agreement and investment policy statement before funding your account.
